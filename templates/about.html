Our Mission

Welcome to New2Canada, your personalized guide to settling into life in Canada. We know that moving to a new country can be overwhelming, so we created this application for the SFU Surge Stormhacks 2025 hackathon to simplify your journey.

Our core mission is to help you navigate the essentials and feel at home in Canada faster. New2Canada connects you to the specific information you need, right when you need it, by leveraging a smart chatbot tailored to your unique situation.
